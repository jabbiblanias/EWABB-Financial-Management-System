About EWABB

The Empowered Women Association of Barangay Bañadero (EWABB) is dedicated to providing financial support and opportunities to women. Through innovative banking services, EWABB fosters savings, loans, and investments for stronger communities.

Our Services

Savings

Secure and grow your money with easy savings plans.

Loans

Flexible and accessible loans tailored to your needs.

Investments

Smart investment options to secure your future.

Our Programs

EWABB supports women through dedicated initiatives that strengthen financial independence and community growth.

Microfinance

Accessible loans that give women the power to start and sustain small businesses.

Skills Training

Workshops and mentorship to help women gain knowledge, confidence, and opportunities.

Community Projects

Collective efforts that invest in the barangay’s growth and empower future generations.
